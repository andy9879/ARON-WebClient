ARON


  This network revolves around the observation of the marine environment in three dimenions through in situ subsurface sensors. This network aims to expand the gap created in remote sensing of aquatic systems by filling a niche that satallite data cannot exploit - oceanic and littoral phenomena at depth.

Santa Cruz Harbour



This man-made harbor is a predictably turbid environment; the sediment of the harbor is composed of fine silt and clay. Though the harbor is protected from large swell, the tides have a dramatic effect on the clarity of the water. Such predictable suspended particulate matter fluctuations made this our ideal test site for the turbidity meters over intermediate time scales.
